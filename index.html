zapLink — Share files over Wi-Fi. No cloud, no cables.

Zaplink turns your computer into a private local server. Pick a folder, hit Start, and any phone or Smart TV on the same Wi-Fi network can open it in a browser — instantly. Free for macOS, Windows & Linux.

How it works

  1. Pick a folder on your computer you want to share.
  2. Hit Start — a local server spins up instantly with a fresh PIN code.
  3. Any device on the Wi-Fi scans the QR code, enters the PIN, and is in.

Security

A 4-digit PIN gates every visitor, and you can see — and revoke — every connected device in real time.
